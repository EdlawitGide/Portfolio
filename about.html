Get to Know Me


I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Information Technology with a Minor in Financial Planning, with an expected graduation in December 2026. My passion for technology and problem-solving developed later in my academic journey, but since discovering it, I’ve been fully immersed. I love how technology can be used to solve real-world problems, and I find the process of creating solutions both meaningful and rewarding.


Throughout my studies, I have gained practical experience in both front-end and back-end development through a variety of hands-on projects. These opportunities have allowed me to apply my knowledge in real-world contexts and sharpen my problem-solving skills. I thrive on the challenge of building systems that are both functional and visually appealing, and I find it fulfilling to see my ideas come to life.


In addition to my coursework, I have worked on several full-stack web applications, designed data visualizations, and applied predictive models. I am passionate about working on impactful projects and am particularly interested in Software Engineering, Product Management, Machine Learning, and FinTech. I am always eager to expand my knowledge and am actively seeking internship opportunities where I can learn, grow, and make valuable contributions.


Outside of my academic work, I enjoy spending quality time with friends and family, who are an important source of support and motivation. I am also an avid listener of R&B music, which helps me unwind, and I love traveling to explore new places and immerse myself in different cultures.
